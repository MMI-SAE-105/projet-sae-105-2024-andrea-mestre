The Visionary of the Future

David Cronenberg

By Andréa Mestre

Published on 22/10/2150 at 6pm

Explore the fascinating universe of David Cronenberg, a visionary filmmaker whose works shaped our understanding of technology and the human psyche.

Let yourself be inspired by his thought-provoking ideas and his lasting influence on past contemporary culture.

Cronenberg's Influence on Technology and the Psyche

Through films like Videodrome and eXistenZ, Cronenberg anticipated the psychological dilemmas related to human interaction with technology.

Our research indicates that these works are deep explorations of the human condition in the face of technological objects.

David Cronenberg

Quote from an Old Document: "Technology can become a second skin, transforming our perception of the world." - David Cronenberg

Analysis of Key Works

Videodrome: In this film, Cronenberg highlights the harmful effects of media on the human psyche. For our archaeologists, Videodrome perfectly illustrates the potential dangers of the CPI, which we consider a vector of psychological control rather than just an entertainment tool.

Videodrome, David Cronenberg

eXistenZ: This film addresses the question of video games as immersive realities. Our analysis suggests that Cronenberg anticipated the profound implications of human interaction with the CPI, presenting it as an interface that could alter the perception of reality and trigger unexpected behaviors.

eXistenZ, David Cronenberg
