About

>

My name is Andréa Mestre, I am a first-year student in the Multimedia and Internet Professions program at the MMI department of Montbéliard.

This website was created as part of a university project on the topic "Prehistory of the Future". As you can see through my website, it presents a technological object from our daily lives, the video game console, through the eyes of people from the future. The texts were fully generated by an AI.

Glossary

Psyche

The set of conscious and unconscious processes specific to each individual.

Therapeutics

Part of medicine that deals with medicinal, surgical, or other means to heal or relieve diseases.

Simulation

A virtual representation of a human activity or the functioning of a machine, where the player feels physically involved in the activity displayed on the screen.

Interactive system

An interactive system is a computer application that takes into account, during its execution, information communicated by the user(s) of the system.
